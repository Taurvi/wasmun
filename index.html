Director General (DG)

  • Acquiring the staff to help to run committees.
  • Decides what the committees will be, along with topics (with the SG).
  • Ensures the staff are appropriately trained to handle their committees.
  • Making rounds on the conference day between the committees.
  • Assisting in resolving disputes.
  • Edits the background guides, ensure the staff upholds the same high standards WASMUN is known for.

Assistant Director General (ADG)

  • Assisting Director-General with edits for Background Guides, Position Papers.
  • Assisting in communication with dais.
  • Assisting DG in managing dais (hiring, communication, briefing/training).
  • Rounds on day of conference between committees.
  • Resolving rule disputes during conference.
  • In charge of crisis, if implemented.

Under-Secretary General of Public Relations (USGPR)

  • Maintaining blog posts and Facebook pages.
  • Distribution of promotional materials.
  • Networking with local organizations and their events.
  • Networking with possible sponsorship opportunities.
  • Securing a keynote speaker.
  • Outreach to new/prospective schools (via mass communication).
  • Maintaining an updated contact sheet.
  • Providing education to new/developing teams (allocated with other positions).

Under-Secretary General of Finances (USGF)

  • Forecasting revenue.
  • Budgeting of expenses.
  • Auditing school payments and ensuring that payments are processed.
  • Fiscal reporting.
  • Filing taxes with the State of Washington.
  • Tracking of expenses.

Under-Secretary General of Registration (USGR)

  • Creating and maintaining the Country Matrix for the Conference.
  • Creating and maintaining Committee Matrices in coordination with the Director General (DG).
  • Corresponding with schools for registration in coordination with the Under-Secretary General of Public Relations (USGPR).
  • Creating and managing country assignments with the Secretary General (SG).
  • Assist in updating the WASMUN website regarding online registration and payment systems
  • Keeping track of school payments in coordination with the Under-Secretary General of Finances (USGF).

Under-Secretary General of Information Technologies (USGIT)

  • Managing the website.
  • Resolving any technical issues that arise.
  • Anticipating any technological needs for the conference.
  • Establishing Wi-Fi access for the conference.
  • General troubleshooting.
